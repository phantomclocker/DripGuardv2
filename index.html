THE PROJECT

We love ice cream

Introducing Dripguard™ by Cone Creations, a revolutionary product that combines the joy of indulging in delicious ice cream with the convenience of advertising spaces. Imagine enjoying your favorite scoop of creamy goodness while seamlessly supporting your favorite brands. With Drip Guard™, every lick becomes an opportunity for advertisers to reach their audience in a refreshing and engaging way. But that's not all - our innovative design doesn't just stop at advertising; it also serves as a droplet saver, ensuring that every drip of your beloved treat is captured, saving you from sticky fingers and messy mishaps. Say goodbye to wasted ice cream and hello to a tasteful advertising experience with Drip Guard™ - where every bite is an ad and every drop is saved for pure enjoyment.
